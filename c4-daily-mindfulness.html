Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'?

Listening, listening, listening…I apply the mindful listening with my flatmate. He can talk more than 3 mins, that for sure, hard to focus but I try to bring my mind back what he was saying a couple of times.
How did the other person find it?
He felt pretty good and our connection is getting stronger each time I ask( and listening) "how's your day?"
Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?
I didn’t know I was doing “walk meditation” … I walk everywhere and I like to clear my mind listening everything around on my journey to work or back to home.
